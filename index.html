What is UBUNTU?

Together we are moving Forward. UBUNTU is a grand collective Fest for all the events of different departments in Patkar, and we are hoping to see you there soon.

Harmony
Join the synergy of students from every corner of our college as we celebrate as one, embracing diversity, talent, and togetherness. It's not just a fest; it's a collective celebration that transcends departments!
Festivity
Where College Life Comes Alive! Join us for an unforgettable extravaganza, a celebration where students unite to create memories, forge friendships, and revel in the spirit of camaraderie.
